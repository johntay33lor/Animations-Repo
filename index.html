Motivational Quotes





My Go-to Quote

The ultimate measure of a man is not where he stands in moments of comfort and convenience, but where he stands at times of challenge and controversy.

Dr. Martin Luther King Jr.


This quote holds a special place in my heart, hence it being my go to quote. This quote is a reminder for me that it is important to be able to function, even when not in a comfortable situation. I have this quote tattooed on my arm as a reminder.



Quote to Keep Pushing

Don't stop when you're tired, stop when you're done.

Grandfather


I remember my grandfather telling me this so many times when I was a child. He used to always tell me that it was easy to quit when you're tired, never take the easy route. I remember this especially on days I feel too tired to do anything



Quote to Work Hard

Hard work beats talent when talent doesn't work hard

Football Coach


My high school football coach loved this quote so much that he had it placed on every locker in the locker room. This quote means a lot to me because of the truth it holds. I remember this to make sure that another person can ever out work me.
